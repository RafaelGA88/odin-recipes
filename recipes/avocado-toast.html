Avocado Toast

An easy, balanced, and delicious breakfast, lunch, or dinner

Serves one or two

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of your preferred bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, pitted, peeled
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • salt to taste
  • paprika or tajin to taste

Directions:

  1. Step 1
    2.

    Melt some butter in a skillet over medium-low heat. Crack eggs into the skillet side by side and cook until eggs are white on the bottom layer and firm enough to flip, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip eggs, trying not to crack the yolk, and cook until egg reaches desired doneness, 2 to 5 minutes more.

  2. Step 2
    3.

    Meanwhile, toast bread slices to desired doneness, 3 to 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3
    4.

    Mash avocado in a bowl. Spread avocado mixture onto toast. Top with fried egg and season with salt, pepper, and paprika/tajin to taste.

  4. Step 4
    5.

    Enjoy and share with your loved ones!
